🌬 Awnings & Outdoor Furniture

If the apartment seems buttoned up when you arrive, that is normal. Strong Riviera winds can arrive quickly.

  • Retract both awnings before leaving either terrace.
  • Return cushions and covers if you used them.
  • Leave everything the way you found it.

  • Connect to Wi‑Fi.
  • Open the shutters.
  • Turn on AC if you'd like.
  • Drop your bags.
  • Walk to the sea.
  • Relax.

🌿 Main Terrace & Balconies

To the right of the large sliding doors are two rows of metal buttons.

  • Upper row: terrace lights.
  • Lower controls: metal shutters and terrace awning.
  • Left button: Start / Stop.
  • Right button: Up / Down.

Bedroom balconies have similar shutter controls and no awnings.

🌅 Rooftop Terrace

Exit the apartment, turn right before the elevator, and go up one flight. Use the rooftop terrace key.

  • Outdoor kitchen has plates, glasses, napkins, serving items.
  • Bluetooth speaker, TRX, resistance bands, and yoga mats are in storage.
  • Awning remote is in the drawer next to the rooftop sink.
  • Outdoor shower is great for cooling off or rinsing sand.

Please lock both the rooftop door and the stairwell door when you leave.

Our favorite time up here is about an hour before sunset.
❄️ Air Conditioning
  • Three units: one in each bedroom and one in the kitchen/living room.
  • Kitchen unit works best when bedroom doors are closed.
  • Turn off units in rooms you aren't using.
  • Close shutters and terrace doors at night to keep cool and block morning sun.
🛏 Bedrooms & Storage

Primary Bedroom: queen-size bed, full closet, private balcony.

Living Room: sleeper sofa.

Storage: primary closet plus hallway closet with hanging space and fabric shelving. Bathrooms have storage for toiletries.

🚆 Getting Around

🚆 Trains

Menton Garavan station is 3–5 minutes away.

Italy: opposite platform via underpass. Monaco / Nice: platform closest to apartment.

Use SNCF Connect for tickets and schedules.

🚌 Buses

Nearest stop is outside the building to the left. Tap credit card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay. Press red STOP button before your stop.

Most routes stop around 7:00–7:30 p.m.

🚲 Bikes

Across the street, down the stairs to the lower level, turn right for R Bicycles. Traditional bikes about €20/day; electric about €40/day. Feel free to use our helmets.

🚕 Uber & Bolt

Uber works but can be expensive for short trips. Bolt may be cheaper.

🚶 Walking
  • Beach — 2 minutes
  • Garavan Station — 3–5 minutes
  • U Express — 5–7 minutes
  • Old Town — 20–25 minutes

☕ Coffee Help

Red CALC light means the machine needs attention soon. It appears after about 200 cups; you still have about 30 cups.

  1. Lift out the water reservoir.
  2. Scan the QR code on the back.
  3. Cleaning tablets and descaling powder are on the top shelf above the machine.
  4. Use a large aluminum mixing bowl under the spouts.
