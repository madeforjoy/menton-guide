🚆 Getting Around

Walking, trains, buses, and bicycles make it surprisingly easy to explore the Riviera.

🚆 Trains

Menton Garavan station is a 3–5 minute walk.

Italy: walk under the tracks to the opposite platform for Ventimiglia.

Monaco, Nice & beyond: platform closest to the apartment.

Use SNCF Connect for tickets, schedules, delays, and stored tickets.

For longer trips, book early for better prices and more train options.

🚌 Buses

Nearest stop is outside the building to the left. Tap credit card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay when boarding. Press the red STOP button before your stop.

Google Maps works well for bus routes. Most routes stop around 7:00–7:30 p.m.

🚲 Bicycles

Across the street, go down the staircases to the lower level and turn right for R Bicycles.

  • Traditional bikes: about €20/day
  • Electric bikes: about €40/day

Feel free to use our helmets.

🚕 Bolt & Uber

Uber is available but can be expensive for short trips. We've had downtown rides back cost €20–25. Bolt may be less expensive.

🚶 Walking

  • Beach — 2 minutes
  • Garavan Station — 3–5 minutes
  • U Express — 5–7 minutes
  • Old Town — 20–25 minutes
Some favorite days are just wandering until something catches your attention.

✈️ Before You Arrive

🛂 Passport

Carry your passport for international travel. While sightseeing, we usually leave passports safely in the apartment and carry a clear photo on our phones.

For VAT refunds, some stores may ask for the original passport.

✈️ Arriving in Europe

Arrival can vary dramatically due to biometric entry and immigration queues. Our quickest landing-to-exit experience has been 45–60 minutes; we've heard first arrivals can take 30 minutes to more than 2½ hours.

Don't book a tight train connection after an international flight.
🚕 Airport Transportation

If traveling with several suitcases, we strongly recommend a taxi into Paris. The fixed fare is generally around €55 and worth it after an overnight flight.

For Paris taxis, Uber and G7 Taxi are both useful. G7 taxis may have better access near the Paris apartment.

🚄 Long-Distance Trains

Book early. Reserve seats when possible; we prefer upper level, near luggage storage.

Most Riviera trains leave from Gare de Lyon. Arrive 45–60 minutes early. Platforms often appear only 15–20 minutes before departure.

Bring lightweight cable locks for larger luggage if desired.

💶 VAT Refunds

For larger purchases, ask for a VAT Refund Certificate before leaving the shop. At the airport, scan paperwork at kiosks or use the official VAT refund app where available.

☕ Coffee Machine Cleaning & Descaling

You'll know the machine needs attention when the small red CALC light appears just to the right of the Start button.

The machine reminds you after about 200 cups. Once CALC appears, you still have about 30 more cups.

  1. Lift out the water reservoir.
  2. Turn it around and scan the QR code on the back.
  3. Cleaning tablets and descaling powder are on the top shelf above the coffee machine.
  4. Use a large aluminum mixing bowl under the spouts.

🎥 Official Krups cleaning & descaling video

The process takes about 20–25 minutes including rinse. If the machine pauses, it's usually waiting for you to press Start.
