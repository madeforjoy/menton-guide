Using the Backup Key

The key also operates the European high-security multipoint lock. More than four complete turns is normal.

  • To unlock: insert the key, turn it clockwise through more than four complete turns until it stops, then press the handle down.
  • To lock: close the door and turn the key counterclockwise through more than four complete turns until it stops.

☕ Coffee Help

Red CALC light means the machine needs attention soon. It appears after about 200 cups; you still have about 30 cups.

  1. Lift out the water reservoir.
  2. Scan the QR code on the back.
  3. Cleaning tablets and descaling powder are on the top shelf above the machine.
  4. Use a large aluminum mixing bowl under the spouts.
